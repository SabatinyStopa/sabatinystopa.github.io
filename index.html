Hamster On Rails

Hamster on Rails is a charming puzzle strategy game where players must build a railroad track using a limited number of pieces, guiding a hamster to collect precious ore and a key to progress to the next level. As the sole developer behind the game, I was responsible for creating all systems and gameplay mechanics.

I developed the core gameplay, including the track placement mechanics, interaction systems, and item collection features. The game offers a variety of track pieces, each with its unique function, such as levers to rotate tracks and buttons to deactivate traps. I also worked on the interactive help system, allowing players to receive guidance during gameplay and made sure it could be turned off for a more relaxed experience.

Additionally, I implemented features like X-ray vision to help players plan their routes, autosave after each level, and crafted the systems that allow for progressing through 64 levels across four regions.

Hamster on Rails was awarded the Best In Play prize at the 2023 Game Developers Conference, a recognition of its engaging and innovative puzzle design.

Developed on Unity.
